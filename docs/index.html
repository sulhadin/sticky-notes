Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Sticky Markdown is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's what you need to know:

Data Collection
Sticky Markdown does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your notes are yours alone.

iCloud Sync
If you use iCloud, your notes are synced through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is stored in your private iCloud account and is not accessible to us or any third party. Apple's iCloud is protected by Apple's privacy policy.

Analytics
Sticky Markdown does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not track how you use the app.

Local Storage
All note data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. If iCloud is enabled, this data is also synced to your private iCloud container.

Third-Party Services
Sticky Markdown does not integrate with any third-party services. No data leaves your device except through Apple's iCloud sync.

Children's Privacy
Sticky Markdown does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy
If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at sulhadin@gmail.com.
